About Us
Who are we? Buzzniche is a school-based business program built by a group of ABM students from Maria Asuncion Rodriguez Tinga Highschool (MRTHS). This program is composed of integrated businesses owned by the students. Our project aims to expose our entrepreneurial innovations to potential customers outside the campus. This move is led by Ms. Agnes Navarro, a grade 12 adviser.
